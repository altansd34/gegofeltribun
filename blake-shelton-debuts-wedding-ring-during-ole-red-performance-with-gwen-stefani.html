Newlyweds Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani were on cloud nine during a surprise performance at his Ole Red venue in Tishomingo, Oklahoma. This performance marked the first time that fans have gotten to see Shelton’s wedding band up close, and the pair were positively giggly. Stefani even corrected Shelton when he called her by her maiden name.

Blake Shelton Brings Wife Gwen Stefani to Rehearsal at His Ole Red Venue

Shelton initially tweeted that he would be rehearsing at Ole Red on July 15. The Voice coach will be embarking on his Friends and Heroes Tour starting on August 18 in Nebraska. Shelton did not mention Stefani would be joining him for these rehearsals. Fans were given the surprise of a lifetime when they saw the “Slow Clap” singer at the venue.

Shelton strummed the guitar with his wedding band on full display. Stefani’s diamond sparkler has been getting a lot of love online since the couple got engaged in October 2020. Seeing Shelton wearing a wedding band is equally as fascinating. They tied the knot in an intimate ceremony at Shelton’s Oklahoma ranch on July 3.

The couple performed several versions of her song “Don’t Speak” along with their hit duets “Happy Anywhere” and “Nobody But You.” The crowd buzzed with people professing their love for the pair. In one adorable blunder, Shelton introduced his wife as Gwen Stefani.

“I thought it was Gwen Shelton now,” she playfully said.

The Couple Took a Few Days Off Before Heading Back to Work

Once Shelton wraps up his tour, he’ll be headed back to film more of Season 21 of The Voice, airing on September 20. Stefani is busy preparing for her Las Vegas residency at Zappos Theater this fall. They both played into the electric energy of the crowd and gave fans the dose of live music they have been missing.

“They were so happy and sweet with each other, like they were just in a little happiness bubble,” a source told E! News. “He sounded so great and he’s always so kind and generous to the crowd, talking, waving and signing things.”

Though the couple is already back on their work grind two weeks after the wedding, they did take a few days off to celebrate. Many speculated whether or not Shelton and Stefani would go on a honeymoon. It looks like they had a local mini moon instead.

“After the wedding everyone left town and Gwen and Blake spent a few days on the ranch with just her kids,” a source said. “It was really relaxing and they could let everything from the weekend sink in.”
