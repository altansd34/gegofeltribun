Ceelo Green Said He Would Return As A Coach On 'The Voice'

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

NBC

Reading Time: 1 minutes

When The Voice began back on 2011 the original four coaches were Adam Levine, Blake Shelton, Christina Aguilera, and Ceelo Green. After a long absence, Ceelo is coming back to the show as a mentor. But could he come back for an entire season as a coach in the near future? Ceelo would be down with that.

I love me some Ceelo Green. I could listen to that guy sing the phonebook. He was also a great coach on the show because he’s not only a great singer but a producer as well. He originally left the show after four seasons because he got in trouble with the law for a drug charge. Even though he’s coming back to mentor this season, Ceelo would very much like to coach again, please. He told Billboard Magazine,

“I’ve talked very possibly about doing The Voice again. So I just kind of put that out there, wishful thinking, kind of willing it. Because I would love to do it again.”

I hope they do bring him back when Jennifer Hudson inevitably goes back to jolly ‘ol England for The Voice UK.It would be like getting the old band back together. Plus, Ceelo is a really snazzy dresser. I mean, kind of strange sometimes, but definitely snazzy.
