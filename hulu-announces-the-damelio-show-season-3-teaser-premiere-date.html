Hulu Announces 'The DAmelio Show' Season 3 Teaser, Premiere Date

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Christopher Ollin/GC Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

After months of waiting, fans finally know the exact premiere date of the upcoming third season of The D’Amelio Show. The show also released a preview of what’s to come with a clip featuring the D’Amelio’s sisters and Charli’s present boyfriend, Landon Barker.

The D’Amelio Show Season 3 Premiere Date Revealed

For some time, Hulu has been encouraging viewers to catch up on the first two seasons of The D’Amelio Show. Now, after giving fans a teaser, Hulu revealed that season three will stream starting September 20.

In the video teaser, Charli and Landon were giving Dixie a list of guys that might potentially be her boyfriend.

“Landon and I talk a lot about Dixie, you know, finding a boyfriend,” Charli said.

Celebrities in the list included Drake, Shawn Mendes, and Josh Richards. Charli and Landon were obviously enjoying the game, while Dixie seems awkward about it.

Season three of The D’Amelio Show will take viewers behind the scenes of the lives of TikTok influencers Charli and Dixie. The show will also follow the world of their parents, Marc and Heidi. Essentially, season three is expected to explore the said family’s adventures as they attempt to maneuver their newfound popularity.

Charli to Talk About Her, Barker’s Love Story

On August 22, the show also gave a sneak peek of Charli’s love life. For those who may not know, the younger D’Amelio is currently in a relationship with the son of Travis Barker, who is married to Kourtney Kardashian.

“I said, ‘Hi!’ and he said, ‘What’s good?'” she said.

In the video teaser, Charli can be seen asking her boyfriend her permission to tell how they met. Landon has briefly appeared on season two, but it seems that he’ll be having several appearances in the upcoming season.

It’s no new to Landon, though, who had been in a reality show titled Meet the Barkers on MTV. He was a toddler then, appearing alongside his dad, mom Shanna Moakler and sisters Alabama and Atiana.
