God Wanted You, We Will Take Care of the Boys: Late Pastor Bimbo Odukoyas Children React to Stepm

  • The children of late Bimbo Odukoya, Jimmy, and Tolu have shared their heartfelt condolences following the death of their stepmother, Nomthi
  • Jimmy and Tolu shared the emotional post on their Instagram pages and spoke fondly of late pastor Nomthi Odukoya
  • They promised to take care of their dad and the kids late Nomthi left behind as they wished her eternal rest

The family of Pastor Taiwo Odukoya is mourning after his second wife, Nomthi Odukoya died following a hard battle with cancer.

The pastor's older children from his previous marriage to late Bimbo Odukoya, Jimmy and Tolu have reacted to the demise of their stepmom by penning emotional notes.

They offered their condolences on their verified Instagram pages.

Tolu Odukoya mourns

Tolu Odukoya referred to the late Nomthi as an authentic Christian and a woman that truly loved God and God wanted her.
