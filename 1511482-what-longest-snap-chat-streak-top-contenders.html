What is a Snapchat streak?

Snapchat streaks, also known as Snapstreaks, represent the number of days you have sent a photo or video snap to your Snapchat friend. Snapchat's Snapstreak feature was officially announced on April 6th, 2015.

However, it has been reported that some users had already begun maintaining streaks of up to 12 days before this announcement, indicating that the feature was made available to users 12 days before it was officially announced.

The feature is exciting and fun to try. When you and your friend send snaps back and forth to each other at least once in a period of twenty-four hours for three to four days straight, you will see a small fire icon next to that friend’s name. This is a Snapchat streak.
