Ariana Grande has shared how she’s planning to celebrate the 10th anniversary of her debut album, Yours Truly. That includes a new deluxe release of the project, as well as live performances. The celebration kicks off August 25.

Ariana Grande Celebrates 10th Anniversary of Yours Truly

Earlier this month, Grande teased that she was working on something for the 10th anniversary of Yours Truly, which was released in 2013. Then over the weekend, she shared a video on social media revealing what fans can look forward to.

On August 25, Grande will be releasing a deluxe digital version of the album, as well as new live performances of her songs “Honeymoon Avenue” and “Daydreamin’.” The next day, August 26, fans will be able to enjoy part one of a Q&A, as well as new merchandise.

Grande will also be sharing a live performance of her song “Baby I” on August 27, followed by part two of the Q&A and a vinyl pre-order on August 28. Then on August 29, fans can enjoy live performances of “Tattooed Heart” and “Right There.”

The festivities will come to a close on August 30 (the original album’s release date), with a live performance of “The Way,” as well as behind-the-scenes footage.

Grande Recorded Her New Performances in London

According to ABC News, Grande posted about the upcoming live performances on her Instagram stories. She shared that she recorded the performances in London, where she has been filming the movie adaptations of Wicked.

“I can’t wait for you to hear them. This was such a healing and special project to do,” Grande wrote of her experience performing the songs.

It definitely looks like fans are excited about the 10th anniversary, since Grande’s announcement video has over 1.7 million likes on Instagram as of this writing. Are you looking forward to the upcoming festivities? Let us know in the comments below.
