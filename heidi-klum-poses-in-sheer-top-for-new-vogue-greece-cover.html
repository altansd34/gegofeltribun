America’s Got Talent judge Heidi Klum appears on the new cover of Vogue Greece to mark the magazine’s fourth anniversary. The supermodel actually posed for not one but two covers, which she shared on her Instagram account.

Heidi Klum Poses for Vogue Greece Cover

In the first cover photo for Vogue Greece, Klum wears a sheer top with nothing under it. The look is paired with a sleek hairstyle and minimal makeup. In the other cover, Klum can be seen wearing a white jacket and matching underwear.

The magazine teased, “Just a few months before she turns fifty, the famous supermodel is photographed for the April issue and talks exclusively about her career, immaculate style, warm family, and all that makes her happy now.”

According to Entertainment Tonight, the interview includes Klum’s opinion on how the modeling industry is changing. Not only has Klum herself been in the industry for many years, but her daughter Leni is also a model.

“I like that there is now more variety in modeling,” Klum told Vogue Greece. “Back in the day, all the models looked alike, had the same height, the same skinny body, the same hair… Many different types are represented today.”

Klum Is Returning for AGT Season 18

Fans of Klum can look forward to seeing her at the judging table for Season 18 of America’s Got Talent later this year. The model also recently appeared with Simon Cowell and Howie Mandel as a judge on AGT: All-Stars.

Back in February, host Terry Crews seemed to confirm that the three All-Stars judges, plus Sofia Vergara, would be back for this year’s new regular season. When encouraging fans to audition for the show, he included a photo with the four judges and tagged all of them.

In addition to judging AGT, Klum is also the host of Germany’s Next Topmodel. Season 18 of the show is currently airing.
