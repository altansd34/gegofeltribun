Voting Intention: Con 18%, Lab 48% (7-8 May 2024)

Latest YouGov Westminster voting intention figures

The latest YouGov/Times voting intention poll has the Conservatives on 18% (no change from our previous poll on 30 April - 1 May) while Labour are on 48% (+4).

This means that for the second week in a row the Conservatives are on their lowest vote share of this Parliament - although with Labour's vote share increasing they are now farther behind their main rivals than they have been since Liz Truss's brief premiership.

Elsewhere, Reform UK are on 13% (-2), the Lib Dems are on 9% (-1) and the Greens are on 7% (-1).
