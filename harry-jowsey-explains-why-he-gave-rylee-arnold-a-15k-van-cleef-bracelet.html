Harry Jowsey Explains Why He Gave Rylee Arnold a $15k Van Cleef Bracelet

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Photo by: Disney/Christopher Willard

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Harry Jowsey has finally addressed his jewelry gift for Rylee Arnold. For those who may have missed it, the Dancing With The Stars alum gave the pro a ravishing gold-and-diamond Van Cleef & Arpels bracelet over Thanksgiving weekend.

Harry Jowsey Explains Why He Bought a $15k Van Cleef Bracelet for Rylee Arnold

During his appearance on Bru on the Radio, Jowsey opened up why he decided to give Arnold a gift.  According to the YouTuber, he wanted to express his appreciation to choreographer for helping him grow throughout their DWTS journey.

“I wanna get Rylee a gift because she put up with me for two months and she taught me how to dance,” he said. “I learned a lot about myself from being with Rylee … I was like ‘Damn, I’m really grateful and I wanna have a little trophy of her own to look at and remember the time.'”

While the Van Cleef bracelet is clearly his best pick for a gift, Jowsey shared that he took time finding it. He initially chose a pearl bracelet but he wasn’t that convinced. Then, he thought of gifting Arnold a black and silver piece but realized that she “doesn’t really wear that.” He thus ended up choosing “the gold one” which retails for $14,600.

Jowsey went on to explain that his “ego was a little bit hurt” after having three bracelets “on the table.” Hence, he decided to buy all of them and keep the other two for himself.

On Snapchat, the DWTS alum shared the moment he handed the gift to Arnold, capturing her surprised reaction.

“Harry! It’s beautiful. Harry!” Rylee squealed. “Oh my gosh, I’m gonna cry. Oh my gosh. It’s stunning!”

Later, Arnold showed off the bracelet as she got ready. Reflecting on receiving the gift, she said that she’s the “luckiest girl” for having the “most beautiful bracelet I’ve ever seen.”

He Spent Thanksgiving with the DWTS Pro

Amid “crazy” dating speculations, Jowsey and Arnold spent Thanksgiving with her family. In an episode of the Lightweights podcast, the DWTS pro explained why the reality star joined them for the holiday.

“Harry didn’t have any plans for Thanksgiving,” she said. “My family always goes all out for Thanksgiving. All the dinner, all the games. So, I was like, ‘Harry’s got to join. He’s got to have a Thanksgiving this year.’ So, he came along.”

Since their debut on Dancing With The Stars, the duo has sparked romance rumors. Despite fans’ assumptions though, Jowsey and Arnold have consistently denied the same. However, fans began rethinking if the two are not really dating when Jowsey and Arnold spent some time together during Thanksgiving.

“He came to my family’s Thanksgiving. They came down to California to do Thanksgiving here so I could be there, and he joined, and it was really fun,” she said. “He had a good time, I think, and all my nieces and nephew love him, so it was really cute.”

Arnold and Jowsey were eliminated on DWTS during quarterfinals on November 21. Yet, while their stint on the show has ended, Jowsey stated that he and Arnold find time to catch up.

“We’ve somehow found time every single day to see each other,” he said.
