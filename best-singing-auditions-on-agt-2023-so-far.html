It’s no secret that this season of America’s Got Talent has been filled with tons of super talented singers. No matter how you feel about singers taking to the AGT stage, we can all agree that the singers we’ve seen this season are some of the most talented yet.

The Best Singers on AGT 2023

1. D’Corey Johnson

One of the first singers of Season 18 was none other than 11 year old D’Corey Johnson. He came to the stage with a stunning rendition of “Open Arms” by Journey.

2. Dani Kerr

Later in the season, country singer Dani Kerr came to the stage, singing her original “You Can’t Live Here No More.” About halfway through the song, Simon Cowell stopped her, asking for another tune. Her second song was another original, entitled “November.”

3. Trailer Flowers

This country duo was another contestant that was paused by Simon Cowell this time around. The duo came to the stage singing “Country Girl (Shake It For Me)” by Luke Bryan, but it wasn’t enough for the head judge. Later, they continued their audition singing an original, “Daughter of a King.”

4. Lachuné

At the end of the fourth episode of AGT season 18, Lachuné took the stage singing “Yellow” by Coldplay. Judges loved this performance, they even gave her a unanimous yes!

5. The Sharpe Family Singers

The Sharpe Family Singers is a family band that took the AGT stage singing “How Far I’ll Go” from the Disney film Moana. This family showed off their beautiful harmonies and tones, earning a unanimous yes from the judges.

6. Freedom Singers

Vocal group Freedom Singers, hailing from “Skid Row” Los Angeles took the stage singing “Under the Bridge” by Red Hot Chili Peppers. They received an unanimous yes from the judges.

7. Alfie Andrew

12 year old singer Alfie Andrew came to the stage singing Lady Gaga’s “Hold My Hand.” He earned a standing ovation from the judges, they voted him through unanimously.

8. Roland Abate

Filipino singer Roland Abate came to the stage singing Michael Bolton’s rendition of Percy Sledge’s “When a Man Loves a Woman.” All of the judges voted yes for the singer.

9. Putri Ariani

Simon Cowell’s Golden Buzzer Putri Ariani came to the stage singing an original, but was stopped by the judge. Usually, Cowell stops artists because he doesn’t like them. This wasn’t the case at all for Putri, after her second song, he hit the Golden Buzzer.

10. Zoe Erianna

By far, the cutest singer to grace the Season 18 stage was Zoe Erianna. This adorable six year old took the stage singing “Born This Way” by Lady Gaga, and it definitely didn’t disappoint.

11. SAINTED Choir

This unique choir came to the stage singing a medley of Migos’ “Bad and Boujee,” Lil Uzi Vert’s “Just Wanna Rock” and Robin S.’s “Show Me Love.” They were loved by the judges, so they earned four yeses from the judges.

12. Mitch Rossell

Country singer Mitch Rossell came to the AGT stage with his original single, “Son.” This heartfelt song earned four yeses from the judges, and even hit No. 1 on the iTunes Chart the day after the show aired.

13. Steel Panther

Steel Panther is a former rock group that came to the stage to show off their rock roots. On the stage they played their original, “Eyes of a Panther. The audience and judges absolutely loved this performance.

14. Mzansi Youth Choir

Mzansi Youth Choir earned the first ever, Audience Golden Buzzer, during the show’s season premiere. Their emotional rendition of Nightbirde’s “It’s OK” was loved by the audience and judges.
