Season 17 of So You Think You Can Dance is in full swing, and fans can now request tickets to upcoming tapings. If you want to see your favorite show come to life in person, there are several dates available to attend.

How to Get So You Think You Can Dance Tickets

The show tapes at Television City Studios in Hollywood, California. You can find a list of upcoming dates and links to get tickets here. The earliest date is Friday, June 10. There are several more dates listed through June, as well as a few in July.

Tickets for the July shows aren’t available yet, but you can submit your information now. You’ll be asked to provide basic info such as name and date of birth. You can also add your email address to be notified if more dates are added.

Keep in mind that everyone who attends the taping must be up to date on their Covid-19 vaccinations, including boosters if eligible. The show will administer rapid tests on the day of the taping before the audience is filled. Information will be on your ticket.

The Show Has Yet to Announce a Replacement Judge

So You Think You Can Dance is experiencing some shake-ups this season, as Matthew Morrison recently departed the show as a judge due to breaking production protocols. Morrison apparently sent a DM to a contestant, but he denied reports that the message was flirtatious.

Morrison only filmed four episodes of the season, which means this Wednesday’s new episode will be his final appearance as a judge on the show. The series is expected to announce a replacement judge, but there is no news about this yet.

Currently, the judging panel consists of JoJo Siwa and Stephen “tWitch” Boss. Hopefully we’ll find out soon who will take the third seat going forward.
