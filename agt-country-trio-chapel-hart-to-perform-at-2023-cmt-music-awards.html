'AGT' Country Trio Chapel Hart to Perform at 2023 CMT Music Awards

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by Sara Kauss/FilmMagic

Reading Time: 2 minutes

America’s Got Talent standouts Chapel Hart recently announced that they will be performing at the upcoming 2023 CMT Music Awards. The singing trio is set to take the Ram Trucks Side Stage alongside other rising musicians in the country scene.

Chapel Hart Thrilled to Perform at This Year’s CMT Music Awards

Last Tuesday, Chapel Hart happily broke the news to their followers on Instagram. The country trio, who rose to fame during America’s Got Talent season 17, will be singing their audition piece and original song “You Can Have Him Jolene.”

“HOT DIGGY DAWG!!! Will you look at this!!! Y’all’s favorite girls will be PERFORMING ON THE @cmt MUSIC AWARDS!!!!!” she said. “We are really starting to feel like the hard work is paying off!!! We can’t stop screaming!!!”

Chapel Hart will be joining other country singing newcomers in the fan-voted award show’s Side Stage. These musicians include Megan Moroney, Jackson Dean, Nate Smith, Lily Rose, and Avery Anna.

Apart from the said new performers, Chapel Hart will also share the night with co-hosts Kelsea Ballerini and Kane Brown, Blake Shelton, Carly Pearce, Carrie Underwood, Cody Johnson, Keith Urban and Lainey Wilson.

In their Instagram post’s comment section, one fan quoted Lizzo, saying “it’s about damn time” that the trio perform at the awards night. The upcoming CMT Music Awards on Sunday, April 2 at 8/7c on CBS TV.

They Reflected on Their Childhood Photos Amid Success of Glory Days Tour

Since February 17, Chapel Hart has been hitting the road for their headlining concert shows, Glory Days Tour. Amid the looming finale of this tour, the trio went to Instagram to share their thoughts on this milestone. Together with a touching reflection, they posted a collage of their adorable childhood photos.

“Look at these little dreamers!” they wrote. “And look at them now… traveling around in a tour bus playing COUNTRY MUSIC for the greatest fans on earth!!”

The trio went on with their caption and advised their followers to keep on “dreaming” and “grinding.” Before they gear up for CMT Music Awards, Chapel Hart will be making stops in Cedar Rapids, Iowa; Madison, Wisconsin; and Van Wert, Ohio.
