Dylan Sprouse, Barbara Palvin's Relationship Timeline

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Pascal Le Segretain/Getty Images)

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Dylan Sprouse and Barbara Palvin, the power couple that has captured the hearts of fans around the world, have shared a love story filled with laughter, milestones, and a whole lot of chemistry. From their initial meeting to their recent wedding, their journey together has been a rollercoaster of emotions, growth, and shared experiences.

Early Encounters Start Slowly (2017-2018)

It all began at a Harper’s Bazaar party, where Sprouse and Palvin first crossed paths. Sprouse recalled their meet-cute in September 2018. Little did they know that this encounter would mark the beginning of a remarkable love story.

Their romance took a modern twist as Sprouse slid into Palvin’s DMs on Instagram in August 2017. However, it took Palvin six months to respond, attributing her hesitation to her state of mind at the time. Their online connection eventually led to their first in-person meeting, with Palvin flying all the way to China to spend time with Sprouse, just three months after they had started talking. This bold move would set the tone for their adventurous relationship.

The Leap of Faith, They Become an Official Couple (2019)

The leap of faith proved to be a turning point for both Sprouse and Palvin. After spending time together in China, Palvin realized that Sprouse was the perfect match for her. Their feelings were mutual, and on June 16, 2018, they officially became a couple.

Milestone Moments, Shared Experiences (2019)

As their relationship progressed, Sprouse and Palvin embarked on a journey of shared experiences and growth. From meeting each other’s families to attending red carpet events together, their bond grew stronger with each passing day.

They moved in together in January 2019, setting up their first apartment in Brooklyn, New York. Cooking together, attending events, and even sharing their Valentine’s Day plans, their lives became intertwined in every way possible.

A Strong Foundation, Quarantine Connection (2020-2021)

The COVID-19 pandemic brought its own set of challenges, but it also brought Sprouse and Palvin even closer. Quarantining together allowed them to discover their compatibility and the strength of their relationship. They navigated the uncertainties of lockdown with grace and understanding, leading to a deeper connection and a stronger bond.

From Engagement to Marriage (2022-2023)

The pinnacle of their love story arrived in September 2022, when Sprouse proposed to Palvin. While they publicly announced their engagement in June 2023, their commitment to each other was sealed long before.

On July 15, 2023, the couple made their love official in a secret ceremony in Hungary, Palvin’s home country. Surrounded by friends and family, including Sprouse’s twin brother Cole, they exchanged vows and began a new chapter as husband and wife.

A Love That Keeps Growing (2023-Present)

Sprouse and Palvin’s relationship timeline is a testament to their genuine connection, shared values, and unwavering support for one another. From a chance meeting to a lifetime commitment, their love story has captivated the world and inspired others to believe in the magic of love. As they continue their journey as a married couple, there’s no doubt that their love will only continue to grow stronger with each passing day.
