Four in ten millennials have no pension provision

Aug 31, 2024 Four in ten millennials have no pension provision, new research from YouGov reveals. The Bridging the Young Adults Pension Gap report indicates that 44% of 18-34 year-olds say they have no pension provision whatsoever compared to around a fifth of 35-54s (22%) and over-55s (20%). YouGov’s study reveals the extent to which under-35s have poor knowledge of pensions. Approaching three in ten (27%) say they simply don’t understand enough about them in general and even millennials who have a pension aren’t always clear about what it entails – with 14% who do have one not knowing what type it is.
