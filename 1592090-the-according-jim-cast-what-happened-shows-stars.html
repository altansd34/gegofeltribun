The 'According to Jim's cast now: what happened to the show's stars?

According to Jim is one of the best sitcoms of the 2000s. The TV series premiered in 2001 and ran until 2009, and it featured actors, some of whom were just starting their careers. So, where are According to Jim's cast members today?
