American Idol Season 10 runner-up Lauren Alaina married Cam Arnold on Sunday in Nashville. The wedding was attended by hundreds of guests, and the couple included their two dogs in the ceremony.

Lauren Alaina Ties the Knot in Nashville Wedding

According to People, Alaina and Arnold tied the knot at the Schermerhorn Symphony Center in downtown Nashville. Arnold described their nuptials as a “big, loud” celebration. The couple invited 450 people to the wedding, including celebs like Jason and Brittany Aldean and Shay Mooney of Dan + Shay.

Alaina joked that she wanted a small wedding and Arnold wanted a big wedding, so they “compromised.” She added of the huge guest list, “I think it’s just a reflection of how fortunate we are — we have a lot of people who love us and we love a lot of people.”

The couple apparently changed wedding planners at the last minute and put the nuptials together in just a month with Sara Fried of Fête Nashville Luxury Weddings. Alaina and Arnold wrote personalized vows for the ceremony, which was officiated by Arnold’s friend Henry Valentine Geny.

“Our siblings all stood up there with us as we got married. And Cameron’s dad was his best man, which was super sweet,” Alaina told the magazine. “It was a very emotional, very heartfelt ceremony.”

The Couple’s Dogs Were Part of the Festivities

Alaina and Arnold included their two dogs in their wedding ceremony. In photos shared by People, they can be seen posing with the pups, who wore floral wreaths around their necks. The couple’s nieces and nephews were also part of the ceremony.

Alaina wore a gown from Anne Barge, which was custom designed by Shawne Jacobs, the brand’s creative director. The dress had a high neckline with lace details across the chest and arms. Her bouquet, from Forget Me Not Designs, included white roses, scabiosa, calla lilies, agonis, and anemones.

The singer walked down the aisle to an instrumental version of a song she wrote for Arnold called “Breaking Up with Single.” Their first dance as husband and wife was to “Feet Off the Ground” by Brent Cobb.

The couple will be spending their honeymoon in Hawaii, which neither of them has ever visited. Alaina shared with People that they hope to have kids “down the line a few years.”
