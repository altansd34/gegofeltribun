Carolina Herrera Believes Women Over 40 With Long Hair and Wearing Jeans Look Ridiculous

Fashion designer Carolina Herrera has stirred up a lively conversation in the fashion world by expressing her opinion that wearing jeans beyond the age of 30 and maintaining long hair after 40 is perceived as “unclassy.” With her reputation for elegant designs and discerning taste, Herrera’s statement has sparked a passionate debate about dressing appropriately for one’s age and expressing individual style.

Carolina Herrera is renowned as a highly esteemed designer in the fashion industry.

PHOTOlink / Everett Collection / East News, Gregorio T. Binuya / Everett Collection / East News

Carolina Herrera exudes timeless elegance and natural poise, which has defined her fashion empire for over 30 years. Her inspiring ascent in the competitive fashion world began when she established her eponymous brand in New York. Today, Carolina’s esteemed fashion collection, including a bridal line, luxurious fragrances, and exquisite accessories, can be found in numerous stores worldwide, contributing to her remarkable success and substantial revenue.

She gave up jeans, mini-skirts, and bikinis.

Wiese / face to face / FaceToFace / REPORTER / East News

The designer shares her personal preference, stating that she opts not to wear mini-skirts or bikinis as she believes they are more suitable for younger individuals. She also expresses her view that wearing jeans after 30 may not align with her style. While her statements have sparked varied reactions among her female colleagues, fashion choices are ultimately subjective and should be based on individual comfort and self-expression.

The designer thinks women over 40 look ridiculous with long hair.

Carolina Herrera has expressed her belief that women over 40 should opt for short hair, as she considers it more graceful and fitting for their age. She explained, “A woman must age graciously and not try to be an age she’s not, or she will look ridiculous. I see many women in the street and, from the back, they look very nice with their long hair and their little skirts. But when they turn around — aargh, they’re ancient!”

Celebrities like Jennifer Lopez, Beyoncé, and Kim Kardashian look as fabulous as ever.

While Carolina Herrera suggests opting for short hair in your 40s, celebrities like J. Lo, Beyoncé, and Angelina Jolie continue to rock long hair and look absolutely stunning. Ultimately, the key is to feel comfortable and confident in your body, embracing the beauty you see in the mirror. Personal style choices should be driven by self-love and what makes you feel your best.

She believes that as time passes, women should evolve and embrace change.

Carolina firmly believes that trying to appear youthful actually ages a woman. She emphasizes the significance of cultivating one’s personal style as they age, stating, “I don’t believe in trends because trends make it look like you’re wearing a uniform. Some item of clothing comes out — and suddenly everyone looks the same.”

Discovering your personal style is easier than you think. While fashion often comes with rules, you can break them. However, being aware of common fashion mistakes that many women tend to make can be helpful.
