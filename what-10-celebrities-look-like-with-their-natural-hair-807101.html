10+ Celebs Whose Natural Hair Is Absolutely Stunning as Their Iconic Look

Hair, besides being an attribute that highlights one’s beauty, has become an important expression of our personality. Brown, blonde, fantasy colors, long or short; the options are endless when it comes to giving a twist to our appearance and finding the ideal look. In the case of some celebrities, they’ve become very comfortable with their changes.

1. Ariana Grande changed her style after destroying her hair.

The singer has been wearing her signature ponytail and straight hair for so long that very few remember her natural hair from her early days. Ariana Grande’s real hair is curly and much darker than we are used to seeing.

The star revealed that she adopted her iconic hairstyle after appearing in the series Sam & Cat, as she constantly bleached and dyed her hair red to play Cat, which caused extreme damage to her hair.

2. Sarah Jessica Parker has been blonde for over 25 years.

Throughout her career, the Sex and the City star has changed the length and exact shade of her hair many times, but she has stayed true to blonde. Her natural hair is actually brown and wavy. The actress has shown that she is not afraid of opinions and changes, and has shown the world that gray hair also goes great with blonde.

3. Nicole Kidman is a natural redhead thanks to her roots.

There are probably few people like Nicole Kidman, whose blonde hair color looks so natural that we don’t even imagine it’s dye.

However, the actress is the owner of spectacular natural curls with a reddish tone due to her Irish roots. Over the years, the Big Little Lies star has transformed her image on multiple occasions to get into the skin of her characters.

4. Jennifer Aniston found her ideal look more than 2 decades ago.

The Friends star left her natural brown hair behind many years ago in favor of a golden blonde with different shades of highlights. To keep her mane healthy and shiny, Aniston trims her ends every 6 weeks and avoids applying heat to her hair when she’s not filming to prevent damage.

5. Gwen Stefani is the platinum blonde queen.

When we think of the perfect platinum blonde, the first name that comes to mind is Gwen. The singer has naturally brown hair; however, more than 2 decades ago, she found platinum to be her color. Since then, she has refused to give it up.

To keep her tone vibrant and flawless, The Voice judge has to dye her hair every week, in addition to using special conditioners that maintain the vitality of her mane after all the chemical processes and heat treatments.

6. Sofia Vergara and the dilemma of being a natural blonde latina

Something that has always characterized the Colombian beauty is her long, silky hair. However, her color has undergone some changes over the years. Sofia’s natural shade is a light golden blonde, but the actress opted to darken it a bit and choose more brown tones.

According to Sofia herself, this helped her to get more roles, as her blonde hair did not fit the Latina profile that most Hollywood productions were looking for.

7. Kristen Stewart’s hair and her chameleon-like transformations

s_bukley / Depositphotos, Peter Brooker / REX / East News

Kristen, without a doubt, is one of those actresses who are not afraid of change. The Twilight star has experimented with so many different styles that it’s hard to know what her natural hair color is.

For the iconic vampire saga, she dyed her hair dark brown and later surprised us with a short platinum blonde cut. So what is her natural color? Images from one of her first films, Panic Room, revealed that Stewart is a natural blonde with an ash shade.

8. Reese Witherspoon is not really “legally blonde.”

PopularImages / Depositphotos, 0000001 / Reporter / East News

Reese is one of the most beloved blondes in Hollywood, especially after her participation in Legally Blonde. However, her natural hair color is light brown. What we know for sure is that platinum blonde looks great on her, and she probably won’t be giving it up anytime soon since even for her character in The Morning Show, for example, the actress wore a brunette wig.

9. Jennifer Lopez went for youthful tones.

Jean_Nelson / Depositphotos, Photo By Michael Ferguson / PHOTOI / agefotostock / East News

The Bronx diva is a fashion icon and has undoubtedly become one of the trendsetting celebrities in terms of hair and makeup. Over the years, she has changed her hair color, opting for golden brown and honey blonde tones with highlights to rejuvenate her face.

Her natural hair, however, is very different. Her tone is a very dark brown and, as we can see, she has beautiful natural curls.

10. Leighton Meester found her ideal color thanks to Gossip Girl.

PopularImages / Depositphotos, s_bukley / Depositphotos

We can’t deny that brunette shades go great with Leighton Meester’s eyes and skin color; however, she’s actually a natural blonde. The actress dyed her hair for her iconic role as Blair Waldorf on Gossip Girl and has refused to go back to her shade ever since. Although we have seen her go blonde on a few occasions, Meester always goes back to brown.

11. Emma Stone’s hair hasn’t always been red

We all used to think that Emma Stone is a redhead. But her natural hair color is blonde. She dyed it in red for the movie Superbad.
