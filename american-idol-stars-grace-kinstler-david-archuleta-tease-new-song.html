'American Idol' Stars Grace Kinstler, David Archuleta Tease New Song

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing American Idol

Photo by Michael Tullberg/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In a virtual exchange, two American Idol sensations, Grace Kinstler and David Archuleta, left fans buzzing with excitement as they provided a sneak peek into Kinstler’s latest release, a song titled “Not Me It’s You.” The duo, known for their powerhouse vocals and undeniable stage presence, showcased their camaraderie and shared a glimpse of the upcoming track, leaving audiences eagerly anticipating its drop.

Grace Kinstler, David Archuleta Team Up To Tease a New Song’s Release

The video exchange, which captured a candid and playful conversation between the two artists, highlighted the relatable theme of the song – parting ways with a toxic friend. Kinstler, with her signature soulful voice, and Archuleta, the American Idol Season 7 runner-up with an angelic tone, engaged in a musical dialogue that beautifully conveyed the essence of the forthcoming track.

“This friend keeps calling me, and it’s only when he needs something. It’s driving me crazy,” Archuleta said.

Their harmonious exchange of lyrics perfectly encapsulated the sentiment of being taken for granted by a friend. A climactic moment in the exchange came when Kinstler said, “And yes, now we drop the bomb” leading to the chorus. The snippet provided a taste of the upcoming song’s chorus, leaving fans craving more.

As fans await the official release of “Not Me It’s You,” on August 4, the teaser video featuring Kinstler and Archuleta has certainly hyped up its release. American Idol, the show they both found success in, continues to produce exceptional talents who continue to wow audiences with their ability to connect and create memorable moments.

Grace Kinstler Encourages Fans To Presave Her New Song

Amidst the growing excitement around her upcoming release, Grace Kinstler took to social media to personally engage with her dedicated fanbase. In a heartfelt Instagram post, the American Idol standout shared her excitement and also revealed that her much-anticipated track, “Not Me It’s You,” is set to grace the airwaves on August 4.

In the brief yet exciting video clip, Kinstler’s charm shined as she addressed her followers with a warm greeting. Her announcement of the release date resonated with excitement, urging fans to mark the date for the highly anticipated drop.

With an inviting smile, Kinstler then directly addressed her followers, urging them to take action by presaving the song using the link provided on her page. Kinstler’s genuine enthusiasm certainly shone through as she expressed her eagerness for fans to experience the song in its entirety.
