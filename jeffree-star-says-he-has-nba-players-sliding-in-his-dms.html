Cosmetic mogul and YouTuber Jeffree Star is no stranger to being in the spotlight or taking down trolls on social media. This time, he clapped back at a person suggesting that he is tall enough to play in the NBA and is “playing with the wrong balls.” Star is openly gay and said he currently has three NBA players in his DM’s.

Fans Take To The Comments

While some fans of Star were in the comments supporting the shade that he threw, other’s questioned whether or not he was telling the truth. Several people replied “spill the tea Jeffree,” wanting him to release the names of the players. Rapper Waka Flocka Flame commented a laughing face emoji on the post as well.

Jeffree Star Recently Went Through A Breakup A Tough Break-up

Star and boyfriend Nathan Schwandt dated for five years before calling it quits on their relationship earlier this year. He announced it in a YouTube video where he was visibly emotional and still dealing with the changes. Star explained that he and Schwandt need to focus on themselves.

Schwandt was never in the relationship for fame or attention. He made it clear that he just wanted to support Star and his cosmetic line. Schwandt often appeared in Star’s Youtube videos and even had makeup products from Star’s line named after him.

Is Jeffree Dating Anyone New?

Many fans speculated that Star was dating fellow Youtuber Mmmmitchell after it appeared that they were spending a lot more time together after his breakup. In a recent Youtube video doing a makeup tutorial using Star’s new Blood Lust Eyeshadow Palette, they cleared up this speculation.

Star made it clear that the two are just friends and stay in separate rooms when they are together. He also says that he is not going to be dating anyone so soon after his breakup.
