Livia Faith, The Voice Season 14 standout and former Team Alicia member, just announced she is pregnant! The 18-year-old singer from Kentucky made waves last year after her three-chair turning Blind Audition. Now, she is due to have “Baby Parks” in August.

See the cute announcement for yourself below!

And if you read the bottom closely, you’ll see that IT’S A GIRL! The “BIGGEST SURPRISE OF THE YEAR” text at the top is an indication that this may not have been planned, but they seem genuinely excited to announce this news. Such an amazing thing to hear from this outstanding alum of The Voice!

Though she was eliminated in the Battle Rounds, you may recall that Livia was in a relationship with fellow contestant and Season 14 finalist, Britton Buchanan. But he is not the father! After being very public about their relationship online, the couple reportedly broke up some time late last summer/early fall. Livia then began posting pictures on Instagram with her new boyfriend Luke Parks around October.

Congratulations to Livia Faith and her boyfriend on their big announcement! We are excited for the young couple and wish them all the best.
