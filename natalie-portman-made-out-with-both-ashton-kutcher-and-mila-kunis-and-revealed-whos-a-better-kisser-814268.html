Natalie Portman Made Out With Both Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis and Revealed Whos a Better Kisse

In 2013, Mila Kunis was voted “Sexiest Woman in the World,” and we all know Ashton Kutcher by his captivating looks and charm. Their chemistry and good looks undeniably place them among Hollywood’s most attractive couples. And while Ashton says he’s the luckiest person alive to have Mila as his wife, we believe Natalie Portman is the one who should consider herself lucky because she got the chance to kiss them both.

Natalie Portman is the only person that both Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher kissed.

Invision/Invision/East News

Natalie appeared alongside Kutcher in the film No Strings Attached, and a year earlier, in 2010, she also shared the screen with Mila in Black Swan. Both roles involved passionate and intense kissing scenes, and while this may not have been a big deal at the time, in 2015, Mila and Ashton tied the knot, making Natalie a person to have kissed both parties in a married couple.

Mila and Ashton both enjoyed kissing Natalie.

In a Reddit thread, Mila Kunis once called Natalie a lovely, polite kisser.

And Ashton also didn’t have any problem making out with Natalie. “I was like, ’Cool! Now I get to go toe-to-toe with Natalie Portman!’ For me, that was the challenge I wanted to take on,” he shared how he felt when he got the script for No Strings Attached.

But Natalie is the one to decide who is the better kisser — Mila or Ashton.

And the title of the better kisser goes to Mila!

When Natalie was asked who was better, she said, “Mila, I mean, obviously!”

But she also revealed that it was awkward kissing Ashton. “It’s awkward! It’s always awkward. It’s just weird to kiss someone that you wouldn’t choose to kiss in your personal life,” she explained.
