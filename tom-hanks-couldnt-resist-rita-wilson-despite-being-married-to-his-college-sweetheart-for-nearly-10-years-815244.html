Tom Hanks Couldnt Resist Rita Wilson Despite Being Married to His College Sweetheart for Nearly 1

Tom Hanks, a beloved and iconic figure in Hollywood, boasts a remarkable and enduring marriage that has thrived over the years. His union with the love of his life, Rita Wilson, radiates joy whenever they are together. Their story seems destined, a true testament to their enduring love.

20TH CENTURY FOX / Album/EAST NEWS

In 1978, Tom Hanks tied the knot with Samantha Lewes. Their love story, though filled with its ups and downs, was a significant part of Tom’s life for nearly a decade. However, by 1987, they decided to part ways, finalizing their divorce. It was during this period that Tom Hanks crossed paths with Rita Wilson.

EAST NEWS, LOIC VENANCE/AFP/East News

Surprisingly, Tom Hanks had his first encounter with Rita Wilson in 1985, while he was still married to Samantha Lewes. Despite the challenges they were facing, there was an undeniable spark between Tom and Rita. In an interview with GQ, Tom fondly recalled that moment when he gazed into Rita’s eyes, feeling an instant and profound connection.

“Rita and I just looked at each other and — kaboing — that was that. I asked Rita if it was the real thing for her, and it just couldn’t be denied.”

Sienna Conwell/Broadimage/Broad Image/East News

After his divorce from Samantha Lewes in 1987, Tom Hanks wasted no time. In 1988, he married the love of his life, Rita Wilson. Their union has thrived ever since, becoming a beacon of love and commitment in Hollywood.

In an interview, Tom and Rita opened up about their enduring marriage, sharing insights into their strong bond and how they continue to nurture it day by day. Their love story serves as a heartwarming reminder that true love can endure, even when faced with life’s challenges.

“The success of our relationship was a matter of timing, maturity, and our willingness to have an intimate connection. When I married Rita, I thought, ‘This is going to require some change on my part.’ I won’t deny that providence was part of us finding each other, but our relationship isn’t magic — the way it’s shown in movies.”

Finding true love is like discovering a rare gem in life’s vast treasure trove—a connection that transcends time and circumstance, filling your heart with unwavering joy and contentment. It’s a journey worth embarking on, where two souls come together, creating a love story that’s uniquely their own.
