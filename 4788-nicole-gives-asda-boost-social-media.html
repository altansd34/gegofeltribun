'Scherzy' gives Asda boost on Twitter

Nicole Scherzinger’s visit to ASDA gives brand big boost on social media

On 3 November, X-Factor judge Nicole Scherzinger went to work in an ASDA with Jahmene Douglas, one of the singers she is mentoring who used to work at the supermarket before entering the talent competition. The former Pussycat Doll donned an ASDA uniform, declared that using a stock-checking machine was “exciting”, and announced over the shop’s PA: “If you vote for Jahmene this weekend, everything is half off in the store.”

While it’s possible ASDA bosses weren’t impressed with Scherzinger’s discount announcement, they may decide it’s worth it for the massive boost the brand received on social media. The day before the X-Factor host appeared at the Swindon ASDA, news about the brand reached only 4% of the UK Twitter population. On the day ‘Scherzy’ strapped on the green and black uniform, messages about ASDA reached the newsfeeds of a whopping 23% of the UK Twittersphere. Top words mentioned alongside the brand that day were: “xfactor”, “nicole”, “jahmene”, “uniform”, and “scherzinger”.
