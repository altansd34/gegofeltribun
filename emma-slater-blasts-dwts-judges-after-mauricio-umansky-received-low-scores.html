Emma Slater Blasts 'DWTS' Judges After Mauricio Umansky Received Low Scores

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Photos by: Disney/Eric McCandless

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Dancing With The Stars pro Emma Slater is clearly confused about the judges’ scoring system. Following Tuesday Night’s episode, the professional dancer shared her frustration over the low scores that she and Mauricio Umansky got during the Motown Night.

Emma Slater Calls Out DWTS Judges for Underscoring Mauricio Umansky

While it’s undeniable that Slater and Umansky have had awesome improvements from the Latin Night, they seemed to have not wowed the panel in the latest episode. The two performed Foxtrot to “Working My Way Back to You” by The Spinners. Afterwards, the dancing duo received a 23 out of 40 for the performance — 7 from Carrie Ann Inaba, 6 from guest Michael Strahan, and two 5’s from Derek Hough and Bruno Tonioli.

The judges’ scores have apparently shocked the professional dancer who believed that The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star deserved better. Shortly after their turn, Slater took to Instagram to blast on the judges’ seemingly unfair scoring.

“I just thought that’s really weird scoring, it’s so crazy. I just feel a little disheartened, because as grateful as I am for the whole scoring, respect it very much. I’ve been on the show for 15 seasons, it’s really hard to watch Mauricio get down on himself, when he’s actually improving and he’s actually dancing really well,” she said.

Slater went on to detail how Umansky “didn’t do a step wrong” in the routine. According to her, it’s been puzzling that they got the lowest score of 5 when Umansky “did really good Foxtrot.”

“I was so confused! He performed that so well, his frame was amazing…his elbows were up and he kept his head to the left,” she said. “If you noticed the hand on my back was closed, we worked really hard on things like that.”

It’s not the first time that a DWTS pro got disappointed for getting a low score this season. During the second week, Peta Murgatroyd was caught cursing when she and celebrity partner Barry Williams received a 5 from Inaba.

Fans Agree with Her Sentiments

Soon after hearing Slater’s rant, many fans expressed the same opinions in the comments. A fan stated that only Inaba gave a fair score for their performance.

“Your facial expressions when they gave you scores is exactly how all of us were feeli[n]g,” one fan wrote.

“So underscored I was shocked,” another fan wrote.

“So confused with the scores tonight!!! He did great!! Def deserves higher than 5 and 6,” another person commented.

Meanwhile, some fans were guessing that the producers are to blame. One fan claimed that people in the production might have wanted “some controversy and drama for entertainment purposes.”

Despite the low scores, Slater is nonetheless keeping her spirits high. In the caption of her live, she emphasized the importance of voting. Subsequently, she encouraged their supporters to help them stay longer in this season.

“VOTE if you can, it really makes ALL the difference in staying in the competition. My partner @mumansky18 killed it tonight, adore him and his spirit and definitely his Foxtrot,” she wrote.

Dancing With The Stars airs on Tuesdays at 8 p.m. ET/5 p.m. PT on ABC and simulcasts on Disney+.
