The Voice Recap: Season 15 Live Playoffs

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Tonight we kicked off the first night of The Voice live shows and they didn’t disappoint. On Monday’s jam-packed show, we had all 24 artists perform one after the other. Who will make it past the playoffs? America will decide based on these performances. Let’s get into it!

Team Blake 

loading="lazy"

Michael Lee

Michael takes the very first performance of the night singing the song Every Time I Roll The Dice.

loading="lazy"

Dave Fenley

Performing to the song Angel Flying Too Close to the Ground, Dave takes a softer approach for this performance and shows his range.

loading="lazy"
