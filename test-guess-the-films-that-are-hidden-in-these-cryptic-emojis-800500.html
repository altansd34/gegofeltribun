Test: Guess the Films That Are Hidden In These Cryptic Emojis

Some films have a way of staying in our memory forever — because of the overall plot, a scene, or even the music or scenery. But are you able to recognize a movie just by looking at emojis? Here they can represent the name, the characters, or some part of the story.

At Bright Side, besides being movie lovers, we love these tests. So we made a new selection of famous films from different genres to see if you can identify them all, (using as a reference only these emojis that we took from emojipedia). Remember that to see if your answer is correct, you have to touch or click on the image. Lights! Camera! Aaaand let’s get started!
